Our Story

Baladi means
my homeland.

We didn't open a restaurant. We opened a piece of Syria. Baladi — the Arabic word for "my homeland" — says everything about why we exist. We missed the food. We missed the warmth. So we brought it to Nairobi.

Find us at Stellato Mall in Westlands. Every dish is made from scratch, every single day. And Syrian tea is always on us.
